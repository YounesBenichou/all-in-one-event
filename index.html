All in one

All In One a unique and a very exclusive event, true to its name, brings together all Esi clubs in one and only place where they can work and collaborate with each other in an atmosphere full of enthusiasm and competition spirit. During 3 days , participants will be gathered to learn many new things and develop their skills thanks to a series of workshops, talks and even conferences hosted by the different participating clubs with qualified speakers and trainers.
